How to Cook Pork Giniling

Pork Ginililing is a dish in the Phlippines which is essentially made from cooking ground pork mixed with diced carrots and potatoes

Ingredients:

  • Ground Pork: 1 KG
  • Potatoes: 2 Pcs
  • Carrots: 2 Pcs
  • Garlic: 4-5 Cloves
  • Onion: 1 Pc
  • Soy Sauce: 6 Tablespoons

Preparation:

  1. Add Oil in heated pan.
  2. Add minced garlic, cook until brown.
  3. Add minced onions, stir for 30 seconds.
  4. Add the ground pork, let it cook, stir ocassionally.
  5. Add the diced carrots, mix then cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Add the diced potatoesl, mix then let it cook.
  7. Add soy sauce, mix well.
  8. Put it in a plate and serve.

Cooking Tips:

  • Alterntively, you can out oyster sauce instead of soy sauce.
  • You can also separately cook the diced carrots and potatoes using water before adding it to the meat to make it more soft
