Bruiser Strategy

Bruiser heroes are durable fighters that deal consistent damage and survive longer in battles. They are ideal for frontline combat and maintaining pressure against opponents.

Defender Strategy

Defenders serve as the main tanks of the team. They absorb damage and protect fragile allies such as mages and marksmen.

Stargazer Strategy

Stargazers gain energy faster which allows them to cast their abilities frequently. This role is useful for controlling fights with powerful skills.
