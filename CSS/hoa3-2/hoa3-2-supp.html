Magic Chess Synergy Guide

About Magic Chess

Magic Chess is a strategy game mode in Mobile Legends where players build teams using heroes with different synergies. Combining factions and roles properly allows players to gain powerful bonuses and improve their chances of winning.

Factions

Some important Magic Chess factions include:

  • Mystic Meow
  • Exorcist
  • KOF
  • Luminexus
  • Toy Mischief
  • Mortal Rivals
  • Soul Vessels
  • Heartbond
  • Neobeast
  • Glory League
  • Beyond the Clouds

Roles

Heroes in Magic Chess also have roles that define their combat abilities.

  • Defender
  • Dauntless
  • Weapon Master
  • Marksman
  • Stargazer
  • Swiftblade
  • Mage
  • Phasewarper
  • Scavenger
